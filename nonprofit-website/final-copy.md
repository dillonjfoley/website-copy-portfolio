## Mission Section

## Every Child Deserves the Chance to Read

Reading is more than a school skill. It affects confidence, learning, communication, and future opportunity.

But many children do not have steady access to books, reading support, or one-on-one help.

Our nonprofit works with families, schools, and community partners to give children the tools they need to become stronger readers.

---

## What We Do Section

## Free Literacy Support for Local Families

We help children build reading skills through simple, caring, and consistent support.

### Reading Tutoring

Volunteer tutors work with children one-on-one or in small groups to practice reading, build vocabulary, and grow confidence.

### Book Donations

We provide free books so children can build home libraries and keep reading outside the classroom.

### After-School Reading Programs

Our programs give students a safe and supportive place to read, learn, and receive extra help.

### Family Literacy Events

We host community events that help parents and caregivers support reading at home.

## Impact Section

## Small Moments Create Big Change

A child who reads with more confidence is more likely to participate in class, finish assignments, and enjoy learning.

A donated book can become a bedtime story.

A volunteer hour can become the moment a child realizes, “I can do this.”

Together, we can help more children experience that moment.
