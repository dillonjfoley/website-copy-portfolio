# Before and After Copy Sample

## Weak Version

We are a nonprofit that helps kids read. We offer tutoring, books, and programs for children. Reading is important, and many kids need help. Please donate or volunteer today so we can help more children.

## Problems With the Weak Version

The weak version explains the basic mission, but it does not create a strong emotional connection.

Main issues:

- It sounds generic
- It does not explain why reading matters
- It does not show the human impact of the work
- It does not build enough trust
- It does not speak to different audiences like donors, volunteers, parents, and schools
- The call to action is clear but not very inspiring

## Improved Version

## Help a Child Read. Help a Child Rise.

Every child deserves the chance to read with confidence.

Our nonprofit provides free reading tutoring, books, and literacy programs for children who need extra support. We work with families, schools, volunteers, and community partners to help children grow one page at a time.

A donated book can become a child’s first favorite story.

A volunteer hour can become the moment a child realizes, “I can do this.”

A stronger reader can become a more confident student.

Donate, volunteer, or partner with us to help more children build the reading skills they need for school and life.

## Why the Improved Version Works

The improved version is stronger because it connects the nonprofit’s work to real outcomes.

Instead of only saying that reading is important, it shows how reading support can change a child’s confidence, education, and future.

It also gives donors and volunteers a clear reason to act by showing that even small contributions can make a meaningful difference.

The improved version works because it is:

- More emotional
- More specific
- More action-focused
- More hopeful
- More memorable

The headline, “Help a Child Read. Help a Child Rise,” gives the nonprofit a stronger message that can be used across the website, donation pages, and marketing materials.
