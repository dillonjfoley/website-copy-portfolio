# Project Brief: Nonprofit Website Copy

## Project Goal

Create website copy for a nonprofit organization that provides free reading support, books, and tutoring for children in underserved communities.

The goal is to help visitors quickly understand the mission, trust the organization, and take action by donating, volunteering, or requesting support.

## Business Context

The nonprofit helps children improve their reading skills through after-school tutoring, book donation programs, family literacy nights, and partnerships with local schools.

Many children fall behind in reading because they do not have enough access to books, one-on-one help, or learning support at home. The organization wants its website to clearly explain the problem, show the impact of its work, and make it easy for people to get involved.

## Offer

The core offer is:

Free literacy support for children and families who need extra help.

Supporting offers include:

- One-on-one reading tutoring
- Free book distribution
- After-school reading programs
- Family literacy events
- Volunteer opportunities
- Donation options
- School and community partnerships

## Copy Objective

The website copy should:

- Explain the nonprofit’s mission in simple language
- Show why childhood literacy matters
- Build trust with donors, parents, schools, and volunteers
- Make the cause feel urgent but hopeful
- Encourage donations and volunteer sign-ups
- Help families understand how to request support
- Make the organization feel caring, credible, and community-focused
