# Project Brief: Ecommerce Brand Homepage Copy

## Project Goal

Create homepage copy for an ecommerce clothing brand that sells comfortable, sustainable everyday basics online.

The goal is to help first-time visitors quickly understand what the brand sells, why it is different, and why they should shop now.

## Business Context

The brand sells premium basics such as t-shirts, hoodies, joggers, and loungewear. Its products are made with soft, responsibly sourced materials and designed to last longer than cheap fast-fashion clothing.

The company wants to compete with large clothing retailers by focusing on comfort, quality, sustainability, and simple style.

## Offer

The core offer is:

Comfortable, sustainable basics made for everyday wear.

Supporting offers include:

- Free shipping on orders over $75
- Easy returns within 30 days
- Ethically sourced materials
- Durable pieces designed to last
- Simple styles that work at home, at work, or on the go

## Copy Objective

The homepage copy should:

- Clearly explain what the brand sells
- Position the products as better than fast fashion
- Build trust with sustainability and quality claims
- Encourage visitors to browse the collection
- Make the brand feel warm, simple, and modern
- Drive clicks to the shop page
