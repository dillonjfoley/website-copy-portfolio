# Creative Brief

## Project Type
SaaS homepage copy

## Fictional Company
ClearPath

## Product Summary
ClearPath is a project visibility platform for growing teams. It helps product managers, operations leads, and department heads see project status, blockers, owners, and deadlines in one shared workspace.

## Business Context
Growing companies often manage work across spreadsheets, chat threads, email updates, and project management tools. As teams get bigger, leaders lose visibility into what is happening. This causes missed deadlines, repeated questions, unclear ownership, and slow decisions.

ClearPath gives teams a simple way to organize project updates, track blockers, and keep leadership informed without adding more meetings.

## Project Goal
Write homepage copy that clearly explains what ClearPath does, why teams need it, and how it helps them move work forward faster.

## Copy Objective
The homepage should make visitors feel that ClearPath is a simple, practical solution to messy project communication. The copy should focus on clarity, visibility, accountability, and fewer status meetings.

## Primary Audience
Product managers, operations managers, and team leads at small to mid-sized companies.

## Main Problem
Teams are busy, but leaders do not always know what is on track, what is blocked, or who owns the next step.

## Offer
A SaaS platform that gives teams one clear place to manage project updates, deadlines, owners, and blockers.

## Primary CTA
Start Free Trial

## Secondary CTA
See How It Works

## Brand Voice
Clear, calm, helpful, practical, and confident.

## Messaging Strategy
The copy should avoid sounding too technical or too broad. It should speak directly to the pain of project confusion and position ClearPath as an easier way to keep teams aligned.

## SEO Keywords
- project visibility software
- project status tracking
- team alignment software
- project management visibility
- reduce status meetings
- project tracking for teams
