# Project Brief: Local Home Cleaning Service Website Copy

## Project Goal

Create website copy for a local home cleaning business that serves busy homeowners, renters, and families.

The goal is to help visitors quickly understand the service, trust the business, and request a cleaning quote.

## Business Context

The business provides residential cleaning services in a local service area. Services include standard cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, and recurring weekly or biweekly cleaning.

The company wants to stand out from larger cleaning franchises by focusing on friendly local service, reliable scheduling, careful work, and clear communication.

## Offer

The core offer is:

Reliable home cleaning from a trusted local team.

Supporting offers include:

- Standard home cleaning
- Deep cleaning
- Move-in and move-out cleaning
- Weekly, biweekly, and monthly cleaning plans
- Free quotes
- Flexible scheduling
- Locally owned service
- Supplies provided by the cleaning team

## Copy Objective

The website copy should:

- Clearly explain what services are offered
- Build trust with local customers
- Show the value of a clean, less stressful home
- Address common concerns about reliability, quality, and safety
- Encourage visitors to request a free quote
- Make the business feel warm, professional, and dependable
