# Before and After Copy Sample

## Weak Version

We are a cleaning company. We clean houses, apartments, kitchens, bathrooms, floors, and other areas. We offer regular cleaning and deep cleaning. Contact us today if you need your home cleaned.

## Problems With the Weak Version

The weak version explains the service, but it does not make the business feel different or trustworthy.

Main issues:

- It sounds generic
- It does not connect with the customer’s stress or busy schedule
- It does not explain why someone should choose this business
- It does not build trust
- It does not make the service feel local or personal
- The call to action is basic and forgettable

## Improved Version

## Come Home to Clean

Reliable local home cleaning for busy families, professionals, and anyone who needs a little help keeping up.

We handle the dusting, scrubbing, sweeping, and wiping so you can enjoy a cleaner home without spending your weekend cleaning it.

Whether you need a one-time deep clean, move-in cleaning, or regular weekly support, our friendly local team makes the process simple.

No judgment. No stress. Just a cleaner home that feels easier to enjoy.

Request a free quote today.

## Why the Improved Version Works

The improved version is stronger because it focuses on the customer’s real need: less stress and more time.

It also builds trust by using words like:

- Reliable
- Local
- Friendly
- Simple
- No judgment

The improved version makes the service feel more personal and helpful. Instead of only listing what the company does, it shows how the customer’s life improves after hiring the business.
