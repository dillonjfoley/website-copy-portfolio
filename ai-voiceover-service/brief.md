# Creative Brief

## Project Type
AI voiceover service landing page copy

## Fictional Company
VocalForge

## Product Summary
VocalForge is an AI voiceover service that turns written content into polished audio. The service helps businesses, publishers, course creators, and content teams convert articles, blog posts, training materials, scripts, and presentations into clear, natural-sounding voiceovers.

## Business Context
Many companies already create written content, but not all customers want to read long articles, guides, or training materials. Some people prefer to listen while commuting, working, exercising, or multitasking.

At the same time, traditional voiceover production can be expensive, slow, and hard to manage. Hiring voice actors, booking recording sessions, editing audio, and managing revisions can take more time than a small team has available.

VocalForge gives teams a faster way to create high-quality voiceover content from the written material they already have.

## Project Goal
Write landing page copy that explains the value of AI voiceover for businesses and content teams. The page should show how VocalForge helps users turn existing written content into professional audio without needing a studio, voice actor, or audio production team.

## Copy Objective
The copy should persuade visitors to request a sample voiceover or start a project. It should focus on speed, convenience, content repurposing, accessibility, and professional quality.

## Primary Audience
Marketing managers, content teams, online educators, publishers, business owners, and course creators.

## Main Problem
Businesses create valuable written content, but much of it is only available in text. This limits how people can consume it. Traditional voiceover production can also be too expensive, too slow, or too complicated for regular content updates.

## Offer
An AI voiceover service that converts written content into natural-sounding audio for articles, blogs, courses, presentations, newsletters, and product content.

## Primary CTA
Request a Sample

## Secondary CTA
See Voice Options

## Brand Voice
Clear, helpful, modern, practical, and trustworthy.

## Messaging Strategy
The copy should make AI voiceover feel useful and approachable. It should avoid overhyping artificial intelligence. Instead, it should focus on the business value: helping teams get more use from their existing content.

## SEO Keywords
- AI voiceover service
- text to voiceover
- AI narration
- article to audio
- blog to audio
- voiceover for online courses
- AI voiceover for business
- professional AI voiceover
